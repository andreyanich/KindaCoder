(1ST) Russian Pelmeni Meat Dumplings 😊

Every recipe made with love

This recipe for Russian meat dumplings, known as pelmeni or peljmeni, is traditionally filled with
ground beef, pork, and sometimes lamb, and served with red-wine vinegar, black pepper, and melted butter.

To make the job go faster, the dough and filling can be made ahead
and the dumplings can be assembled and cooked the next day.

    • LET'S DO THIS!

    Ingredients you need:

      For the dough:
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2/3 cup water (tepid)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 1/4 cups flour

      • For the Filling:
  • 1 large onion (finely grated)
  • 8 ounces pork (lean, ground)
  • 8 ounces ground beef (chuck)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper


    • Steps to Make It

      Make the Dough:

  • Gather the ingredients.

  • In a food processor, combine eggs, water, oil, salt, and half the flour.
    Add the remaining flour and process until a smooth dough forms.

  • Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes or until no longer sticky.
    Wrap in plastic and let rest for 30 minutes. The dough can be refrigerated at this point until ready to roll.


      • Make the filling:

  • Gather the ingredients.

  • In a medium bowl, combine onion, ground pork, ground beef, salt, and pepper until thoroughly mixed.

  • Refrigerate, covered until ready to use.



    • Now cooking!

    Now you have everything you need so you can just form the Pelmeni as you like
    and then just simply cook them in a pan.

    JUST BOIL THE WATER AND THEN PUT THE Dumplings INTO IT AND BOIL FOR A WHILE
